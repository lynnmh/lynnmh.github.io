My Experience

Hello,

I'm Monique. I am skilled in Web Development. Although I haven't been in the field for too long. I have been a self taught individual who used other resources other than
school to practice coding.

One of the sites that helped me learn how to code is W3 Tutorial and Free Code Camp.
W3 helps with looking up ways to use HTML and Javascript plus more.
It explains each syntax for different types of code, and helps you practice.
W3 is great, but my favorite is freecodecamp.
Free Code Camp helped me practice each step of syntax by beginner to expert level.
They also give you a certificate after each different level of lessons for coding is complete.
They are very straight to the point and each lesson is easy to understand, plus well organized.
They also have a comunnity of other students, plus web developers that you can talk to and ask
ask for help. Definitly my go to site.
